Viewport

Viewport is the technical term for the part of a device screen where the content of a publication is rendered.

The viewport and screen are not synonymous. The viewport sometimes occupies the entire screen (e.g., for dedicated reading devices or when an reading system is in full-screen mode), but it all depends on the application.
